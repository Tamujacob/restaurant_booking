Our Story

More Than a Restaurant

Café Javas is a full-service, quick-casual, coffee-themed restaurant chain — the perfect refuge from a busy day's shopping or a quick break from the office. It's where Kampala goes for breakfast, lunch, dinner, coffee and pastries.

We commit to great food, great coffee, great service — an experience that will make you long for more. Each of our 12 locations features a unique ambience with tasteful décor, specially designed for your comfort.
